About

Udaan - your travel expert, Exclusively DUBAI Tours Specialist, company was established in with Headoffice at Bhuj- Kutch. We are specialised in 'Personalized Tours' and Group Tours for Outbound, Inbound & Domestic holidays and International Holidays for individuals, family & cultural groups, corporate.

We have a strong local and International representation in India as our network spread across the Indian and International subcontinent enables us to perform very professionally and effectively. We provide complete range of high quality tourism services.. Our focus on quality of service, transparency and reliability is balanced by our attention to optimizing customer cost, comfort and convenience, thereby ensuring the ideal mix for your travel plans.

Adventure Of Ladakh

06 Nights / 07 Days

2N Leh – 1N Nubra – 1N Leh – 1N Pangong – 1N Leh

  • In Leh, visit Shanti Stupa and Leh Palace
  • Centuries old monasteries in Ladakh– Hemis, Shey, Thiksey, Diskit…
  • Visit & Stay at world’s highest salt water lake – Pangong Lake
  • Drive through world’s highest motorable passes – Khardung-la & Chang-la
  • Stay in Nubra valley and visit to Sand Dunes
  • Airport transfers and local sightseeing tours by private vehicle
  • Start and End in Leh

Amazing Andaman

05 Nights / 06 Days

1N Port Blair – 2N Havelock – 2N Port Blair(Neil Island & North Bay)

  • Light & Sound show in Port Blair
  • Radha Nagar and Elephant beaches, Havelock
  • Session of Snorkeling
  • Scuba Diving (Optional)
  • Excursion to Neil Island & North Bay
  • Airport assistance and sightseeing tours by private air-conditioned vehicle
  • Start and End in Port Blair

Best Time to visit :November to April

Eastern Himalayas

7 Nights/ 8 Days (Ideally)

  • In Gangtok, visit Tshangu Lake & Baba Mandir Excursion
  • Visit, Jhakri Water Falls, Research Institute of Tibetology, Directorate of Handicraft & Handloom and Flower Show
  • In Pelling, Excursion to Yuksom, Rimbi Waterfalls, Rimbi Hydro Project & Khecheopalri Lake
  • In Kalimpong, Dello hills, Flower Nurseries, Jang-Dong-Palriffo Brang and Durbin Dara Hills
  • In Darjeeling, visit to Tiger Hill early morning to view Everest and snow-capped Himalayan range.
  • Sightseeing in Darjeeling - Ghoom Monastery, Japanese Temple , Himalayan Zoological Park & Himalayan Mountaineering Institute
  • Assistance at airport and sightseeing tours by private air-conditioned vehicle
  • Start and End in Bagdogra

Best Time to visit :October to April
